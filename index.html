LRXY is a series of audiovisual art works, a study in minimalism and the intertwined relationship of light and sound. Each LRXY art work is a synchronised audiovisual piece, played out live, consisting of oscillographic visuals projected on a plane. The technical setup brings together vector handling and projection mapping software, modular synthesizers, a modified Vectrex vintage home video game console, cameras, projectors, and speakers. LRXY is designed and realised by Tomas De Rita and Jukka Koops.
